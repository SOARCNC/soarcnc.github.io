About

A precision partner, not a low-cost vendor

SOAR Global Enterprise Co., Ltd builds long-term manufacturing relationships on quality and trust.

Who we are

SOAR CNC — operated by SOAR Global Enterprise Co., Ltd (Taiwan, VAT No. 60345849) — is a precision CNC machining manufacturer serving OEM and export-oriented customers worldwide. We specialise in tight-tolerance turning, milling and turn-mill machining of custom metal parts.

Based in Taichung — a center of high-end CNC machining and machine-tool manufacturing — we combine state-of-the-art CNC technology with Design for Manufacturing (DFM) insight, managing supply-chain complexity so you can focus on your product.

How we work

Our focus is precision, process stability and long-term cooperation — deliberately not low-cost mass production. That means dependable quality, transparent communication, and consistent execution on every international project, from first sample to repeat orders.
